I’m a second-year Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology student at the College of Science and Technology, passionate about programming and problem-solving. My expertise includes C, Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and databases, along with a keen interest in Human-Computer Interaction, probability & statistics, and networking. I have explored network segmentation using Cisco Packet Tracer and enjoy working on hands-on coding projects.

Through CodeInFocus (Youtube Channel), I create text-based coding tutorials and project showcases, focusing on clear explanations without voiceovers or facecam. My goal is to make programming more accessible by sharing insights on software development, networking, and real-world applications. Stay tuned for exciting projects, tutorials, and expert-level content to help you sharpen your coding skills!

What to Expect: In-Depth Tutorials: Step-by-step guides on Java, C, and more. Project Walkthroughs: Real-world projects, from classic games to practical applications. Coding Tips & Tricks: Best practices, shortcuts, and tools to boost your coding skills. Tech Guides: Easy setup for your development environment, from IDEs to programming tools. Whether you’re coding your first “Hello, World!” or tackling advanced projects, this channel is here to help you grow as a programmer.
